collectively imagining radical post-abolition futures

"What shall we build on the ashes of a nightmare?" - Robin D.G. Kelley

This page was created in the soon to be historic era of 2020, when the global pandemic surfaced not only in the form of a diease that attacks the respiratory system, but also as the increased visability of a long standing system of racialized oppression and violence. When Americans heard George Floyd gasp the words "I can't breathe", the last words that Eric Garner had also utter before his murder at the hands of police in 2015, chaos erupted worldwide. The Minneapolis 3rd precinct burned to the ground overnight.
